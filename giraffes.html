GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest living terrestrial animals and the largest ruminants. Their distinguishing characteristics are their extremely long neck and legs, horn-like ossicones, and distinctive coat patterns. They primarily eat leaves from tall trees, especially acacias.

They are native to Africa and typically found in savannahs and open woodlands.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find the giraffes at pen G14, near the central observation tower.
